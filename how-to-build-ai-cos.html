Build These First
The 3 highest-leverage things to deploy.
  • 1
    Email Triage
    A 3-tier sort that runs every 30 minutes. Tier 1 gets flagged for your attention. Tier 2 is held. Tier 3 is archived. You only touch what matters.
    Saves 1–2 hrs/day
  • 2
    Morning Brief
    Every morning, a personalized summary hits your phone. Today's meetings, top priorities, and the one thing that actually needs your decision.
    Replaces the morning scroll
  • 3
    Voice Agent
    A dedicated phone number your COS answers. Screens callers, collects info, books meetings directly into your calendar.
    Replaces a virtual receptionist
